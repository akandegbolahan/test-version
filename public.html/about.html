Welcome to the Oduduwa University Alumni Association

The Oduduwa University Ipetumodu Alumni Association (OUIAA) is a dynamic global network of graduates united by a shared commitment to excellence, growth, and community service. Established to foster lasting bonds beyond the classroom, the Association serves as a bridge between alumni, current students, and the University leadership, championing professional development, social impact, and lifelong support.

Under the leadership of the current Executive Council, OUIAA has evolved into a powerful movement dedicated to empowering members across continents. Through mentorship programmes, career advancement initiatives, educational support, and humanitarian outreach, we ensure that every graduate of Oduduwa University remains part of a thriving family that uplifts and celebrates one another.

We believe in the power of unity, heritage, and legacy. Our mission is to build a network that honors our past, leverages our present, and secures a greater future for all alumni.We are more than an association. We are a global force, passionate about people, purpose, and progress.

Our Mission

To foster unity, collaboration, and shared growth among all alumni

  • To support students and fresh graduates through mentoring, funding, and guidance
  • To promote the values and reputation of Oduduwa University globally
  • To build strategic partnerships that benefit our members and society at large
  • To create platforms for professional development, networking, and innovation.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized alumni community that empowers members to thrive, serve, and lead in their chosen paths, while upholding the legacy and excellence of Oduduwa University.

Our Values

Excellence, integrity, service, and lifelong learning guide everything we do as we build bridges between past, present, and future generations of graduates.

Our History

2009

Oduduwa University Ipetumodu (OUI) was founded

2017

The idea to form a structured alumni body took root, inspired by a vision to create lifelong value for Oduduwa University graduates.

2025

Launched the first alumni scholarship program, supporting deserving students with financial assistance.

2025

Established our digital presence, connecting alumni worldwide through online platforms and virtual events.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register on the alumni portal?
Click on the "Register" button on the homepage, fill in your graduation details and create an account to join the alumni directory.
Can I update my profile after registration?
Yes. Once you log in, you can access your profile and make updates anytime.
How can I view the alumni directory?
After logging in, go to the "Directory" page to browse and connect with fellow graduates.
Is there a membership fee?
Currently, registration is free. However, some exclusive events or contributions may require a fee.
How do I get updates on alumni events?
We post announcements in the News section and send emails to registered members. Make sure your contact info is up to date.
